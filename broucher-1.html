Our Vision

The Spiritual & Social Context

For centuries, Varanasi has been regarded as the gateway to Moksha. Families travel from across India and around the world to perform last rites at Manikarnika and Harishchandra ghats.

Manikarnika Ghat — the eternal flame of liberation. Harishchandra Ghat — the ghat of truth and simplicity.
