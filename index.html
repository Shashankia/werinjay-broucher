Who We Are

Werinjay is a first-of-its-kind cremation assistance service in Varanasi. We support families through one of life’s most difficult journeys with dignity, compassion and professional coordination — from logistics and priests to documentation and NRI liaison.

Mission

To ensure a seamless, respectful and spiritually fulfilling farewell for every family who chooses the sacred ghats of Varanasi.

Vision

To combine tradition and technology so institutions, hospitals and international families can rely on a verified, accountable service at all times.

Spiritual & Social Context

Varanasi’s Manikarnika and Harishchandra ghats have been centers of ritual and solace for centuries. Families often arrive in moments of grief — and need a partner who understands tradition, timings, and paperwork.

Our role is to translate ritual protocols into a reliable operational workflow so families can grieve — not manage logistics.

Challenges Families Face

Emotional Overwhelm

Sudden loss leaves families without clarity. Our trained assistants provide step-by-step guidance so loved ones can focus on grieving and closure.

Logistical Complexity

Wood, priests, ambulance, and permits — all time-sensitive items. We coordinate vendors and paperwork with verified partners.

Authenticity & Trust

Families need assurance that rituals follow correct protocols and verified staff. Our priests and vendors are screened and trained.

NRI Coordination

International families require documentation, livestreaming, and hotel liaison. Our NRI concierge manages everything remotely and on ground.
